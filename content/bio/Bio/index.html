My Story

Before university

I was born in a little village in the north of Italy named Candia Canavese where I attended high school. I was a very active kid, playing sports (football, volleyball, swimming) and spending time with my friends.

University

I attended the BSc in International Economics and Finance at Bocconi University in Milan. It was a wholesome experience thanks to which I learned a lot and defined clearly what I want to do with my life. I did an internship at GS in the securities division in my first year and one at BNP Paribas (in M&A) after graduation.
